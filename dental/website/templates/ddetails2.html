Meet Dr.Md Masudur Rahman Iqbal

BDS,BCS-Health,MS(Oral And Maxillofacial Surgery)

Dr. Md Masudur Rahman Iqbal is a renowned Oral & Maxillofacial surgeon in Bangladesh. After graduation (BDS) from Dhaka Dental College in 1994 he has completed Master’s degree (MS) in Oral & Maxillofacial surgery from Dhaka University in 2009.He has joined Government service (BCS- Health) in 1997 and worked in several hospital of the country over the period. Now working as Associate professor in the Department of Oral & Maxillofacial surgery, Dhaka Dental College, Mirpur Dhaka.

He received an advanced training on Distraction Osteogenesis from Nineth Peoples Hospital Shanghai, China in 2010 and completed a course on basic Microvascular Surgery from Ganga Hospital, Tamil Nadu, India in 2016.

He is working in different field of oral diseases and have special interest in Oral Cancer Surgery, Facial Trauma, Facial Deformities, Facial Reconstruction Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) Surgery and use of Laser in Oral lesions.

Accomplishments

Dr.Masudur Rahman Iqbal is a renowned maxillofacial surgeon in Bangladesh. He completed his BDS from Dhaka Dental College in 1994. He completed his MS in Maxillofacial surgery post-graduation from university of Dhaka in 2009. He received advanced training in maxillofacial surgery from Nineth People’s Hospital Chania and Microvascular training from India.

His special area of interest is maxillofacial trauma, Cancers Surgery and Reconstruction’s. TM joint, Oral Medicine Laser Surgery for Precancers Lesion of oral cavity. Now he is working as an Associate Professor in Maxillofacial Surgery Department of Dhaka Dental college.
