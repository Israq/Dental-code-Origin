Meet Dr.Md.Mahfuzul Islam

BDS,Consultant,Dental Surgeon and Implantologist

Dr.Md.Mahfuzul Islam completed his BDS Degree from Dhaka Dental College in 2003.Then he has completed 1 year post graduate training on Endodontics & Operative dentistry and 4 years post-graduate training for FCPS on maxillofacial surgery from Dhaka Dental College & Hospital. He completed 300 credit hours Dental Implant Education from American academy of Implant Dentistry (AAID) & Surgical training from Bangalore India.

He started Implant Practice since 2007 and have an experience of more than 800 successful Implant Surgery and also working in Laser Dentistry since 2009 as he received advanced laser training from Germany and India. Periodontology is one of his interest area. He is practicing Guided Bone Regeneration Perio - Surgery for last 10 years of his practice. Now he is working as an Executive Director, Dhaka Central International Medical College and Hospital and Consultant Implantologist in Dhanmondi Dental Center.
