Meet Dr. Md Abdul Awal

BDS,BCS-Health,MS(Prosthodontics)

Dr. Md. Abdul Awal is a renowned personality in Bangladesh for his excellent Prosthodontics and Cosmetic Dentistry works as well as an eminent speaker and trainer of this arena of Dentistry. He completed his BDS Degree from Dhaka Dental College in 1994 and finished his post-graduation in MS Prosthodontics from Bangabandhu Sheik Mujib Medical University in 2006.

He has joined Government service (BCS - Health) in 1997 and worked in different hospitals. He received advanced training on CAD - CAM of Prosthesis and Implant Planning from Bengaluru, India.

His interest area of work is Cosmetic Dentistry, Digital Smile Design (DSD), Implant Planning, Crown Bridge and Maxillofacial Prosthesis. Now he is working as an Assistant professor in prosthodontic department of Dhaka Dental College Mirpur Dhaka.
