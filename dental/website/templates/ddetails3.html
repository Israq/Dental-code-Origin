Meet Prof.Dr. Kazi Mehdih ul Alam

BDS, FCPS, MCPS,DDPH,RCS,Fellow WHO:PGC,DPH

Prof.Dr. Kazi Mehdih ul Alam is a Legend in the field of Dentistry in Bangladesh. He establishes pediatrics, Preventive and dental Public Health department in Bangladesh. He completed his BDS from Dhaka Dental College. He was awarded Gold Medal by University of Dhaka for his extraordinary academic performance. He completed his post-graduation from Royal College of surgeons of England as a Commonwealth Scholar . He is founder of Dhanmondi Dental Center. He introduced quality and ethical dental practice in Bangladesh.

He introduced multidisciplinary team approach in a single foot step. He was principal and head of the dept. Children and Preventive Dentistry in Dhaka Dental College. He was former Academic Director Update Dental College and former Principal Pioneer Dental College. He is a WHO Fellow in Bangladesh.

Now he is working Chairman and team leader of Dhanmondi Dental center. He is president of Bangladesh Society of Children Dentistry and advisor Bangladesh Academy of Dentistry international (BADI).
