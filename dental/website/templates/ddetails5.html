Meet Assistant Professor-Dr. Israt Mafruha

BDS (RMC) PGT (BSMMU) MS (Orthodontic)

DR. ISHRAT MAFRUHA achieved her BDS degree in 2004 from Rajshahi Medical College and Hospital, Dental Unit and completed her One-year internship in Rajshahi Medical Hospital and Dental Unit. She had completed a one-year post-graduation training from Shahid Suhrawardy Medical College Hospital on Maxillofacial surgery. Then she had obtained her (MS) degree on Orthodontics in 2018 from Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University.

She had achieved this academic degree after successfully completing 5 years clinical works on patients, performing theoretical exams and research work that had made her specialist, skilled and confident in the field of Orthodontics. Now she is designated as Assistant Professor and is giving her service in the Department of Orthodontics in Dhaka Community Medical College, Dental Unit. Special interest in Interceptive Orthodontics, Demon system and Clear aligner (Invisalign).
